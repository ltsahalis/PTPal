About PT Pal

Revolutionizing physical therapy with AI-powered guidance

Our Mission

PT Pal is dedicated to making physical therapy accessible, effective, and personalized. We combine cutting-edge AI technology with motion tracking to provide real-time feedback and guidance for your rehabilitation journey.

Why PT Pal?

Traditional physical therapy can be expensive, time-consuming, and sometimes inaccessible. PT Pal brings professional-grade therapy guidance to your home, making it easier than ever to follow your rehabilitation program with confidence.

How It Works

Using advanced motion tracking and AI analysis, PT Pal monitors your exercises in real-time, providing instant feedback on your form, technique, and progress. Our system ensures you're performing exercises correctly, reducing the risk of injury and maximizing your recovery.
